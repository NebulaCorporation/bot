Commands

These are all the commands.

?Play

Play a Youtube video, Spotify link or write a song name!

?Invite

Invite our bot to your server!

?Nowplaying

Show which song is playing!

?Pause

Pause the playing song!

?Queue

Show the queue!

?Remove

Remove a song from the queue!

?Repeat

Repeat the last song played!

?Skip

Skip the current song!

?Stop

Stop NebulaBot!

?Help

Get some help!
